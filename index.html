Projects

My Projects

Here are some of the projects I have worked on:

KCCM, Kegeberew Consumer Credits Management

I worked on this project at KTS, primarily handling the entire backend development. The deployed system integrates with the Kegeberew eCommerce platform and is actively used by numerous cafes, restaurants, hotels, and catering services in Addis Ababa. It efficiently processes thousands of orders, manages credits, and handles order tracking.

Kegeberew University LMS Project

Participated as a backend developer in the Learning Management System (LMS) project at Kegeberew University. This project involved creating a basic LMS with integrated tutoring capabilities, allowing different course providers to offer and manage their own LMS systems.

3S Betting Ticket Management Project

Serving as a full stack developer for 3S Betting, I have independently developed a comprehensive betting ticket management system. This project empowers 3S Betting to efficiently manage their business processes, conduct detailed analytics and reporting, record betting ticket outcomes, track wins and losses, process payments, and track results for various games.
